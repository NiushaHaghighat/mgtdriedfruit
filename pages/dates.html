CHOPPED

Chopped Dates is made of Sayer Fruit to be cut into small cubic particles for different uses, and flour of Soya or Rice is added to it to reduce the stickiness. A new innovative product could be a suitable substitution for Raisin in different industries.

KABKAB

Kabkab dates are amongst the most important and abundant date varieties in south Iran. Kabkab dates are considered a wet variety of date due to their high moisture content of around 18%. They are long, oval and around 3.5-4cm in size and vary from yellow to dark brown in color depending on ripeness. Kabkab dates have a soft, fine texture and and a hard skin due to their long, grey pit that clings to the flesh. They are popular for their delicious, sweet, syrupy taste.

MAZAFATI

The most gourmet and elegant moisture date among all. Mazafati is meaty with shiny black skin and sweet taste. The moisture range is between 15 to 35 % that depends to the area and time of harvesting. This type of dates very reach in nutrition values and used during Ramadan for fasting by Muslim countries in order to save more energy during fasting time. Very rich in minerals.

PIAROM

Piarom (Piyarom) is the most precious semi dry date in all. The brown skin and meet are attached which causes a very nice look but with low moisture. Piarom dates also known as chocolate date, very rich in protein and fiber.

RABBI

Rabbi dates are semi-dry, long, oval dates, about 3-5cm in length (but are available in varying sizes). They have thin, meaty skin that clings to the flesh are a soft texture. Rabbi dates are an attractive black colour, and have a unique and indescribable taste. They have a moisture content of around 16%. There is also another, smaller kind of Rabbi Date, which is drier and very hard.

SHAHABI

Shahabi Dates is long in size and is very similar to Piarom. This type is semi dried and has the shelf of 1 year storing at the room temperature. The dates grows in Boshehr region. The coloring of this kind variety is brown and its taste is very delicious.

ZAHEDI

The dry and semi dry date with yellow color to brownish is one of the exported dates from Iran annually. Zahedi date is oval shape and at the end is narrow, very easy to be stored. It can be also used individually and as ingredients. The moisture is about 14 % usually with 18 months shell life in room temperature. Very rich in fiber, Vitamin and mineral.

SAYER

Sayer is in semi dry date category that is very nutritious. It would be yellow at the beginning of growing and turn brown at the time of harvesting. The skin is usually ticker comparing other dates. Sayer have about 16% moisture on harvesting time and the shell life is about 1 year in room temperature. It comes with seed and without seed in three category of select, GAQ and FAQ levels.
