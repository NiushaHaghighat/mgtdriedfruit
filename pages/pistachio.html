AHMAD AGHAEI - ALMOND SHAPE

The almond shape pistachio (Ahmad Aghaei) with Gourmet taste, which is longer than round pistachios. The kernel is red purple and the shell is light cream. One of the famous type of pistachio in dried fruit consumer countries Like India. They will be exported Raw, Roasted & Salted, natural open mouth, mechanical open mouth, and close mouth. Common sizes of ahmad aghaei pistachio: (24-26, 26-28, 28-30)

AKBARI - LONG

The large almond shape pistachio even larger than Ahmad Aghaei pistachio, which is more expensive and is proper for higher level of consumption. Akbari also have high percentage off natural open mouth while harvesting. The kernel is brown purple and the shell is dark cream. They will be exported Raw, Roasted & Salted, natural open mouth, mechanical open mouth, and close mouth. Common sizes of Akbari Pistachio: (20-22, 22-24, 24-26)

FANDOGHI PISTACHIO - ROUND

Fandoghi pistachio is smallest size among all pistachios and growing in round shape. Fandoghi have high percentage of natural open mouth while harvesting, coming with purple kernel and light cream shell. They will be exported Raw, Roasted & Salted, natural open mouth, mechanical open mouth, and close mouth. Common sizes of round pistachio: (28-30, 30-32, 32-34)

KALE GHOCHI - JUMBO

This is a pistachio in round category, which comes in large size and having a rich taste. It creates pretty appearance on a dish. The kernel is red gray and the shell is semi cream. Jumbo Pistachio (Kalle-Ghouchi) will be exported Raw, Roasted & Salted, natural open mouth, mechanical open mouth, and close mouth. Common sizes of Persian jumbo pistachio: (20-22, 22-24, 24-26)
