Company Profile

MGT Dried Fruit stablished in 2004 by Mr. Ebrahim Mirzaei the founder and general manager of the company. We are official member of Iran chamber commerce and member of national dried fruit association in Iran. At MGT, we all come to work every day because we want to solve the low quality problem and wrong nutrition in food industry. Ur mission at MGT is to make sure that people in the word reach the food that is right for them against all improper food products that exist in the market currently. Today we provide the healthiest food that can be consumed as ingredients or individually in all cultures and nutrition principles. We are excited about building strong relationships with everyone we interact with: our customers, our community, and our friends. We have made many friends and customers in many countries like India, China, Taiwan, Sri lanka, Malaysia, UAE, Australia, Swiss, Morocco and many others. We are happy to meet them regularly and enjoy working together.
