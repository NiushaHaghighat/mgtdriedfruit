CLOSE MOUTH

Close Mouth Dried figs come in three different grades, AAA, AA and A. AAA grade of dried figs has a light yellow and white color and the size of them is about 22 mm. Most percent of dried fig in this grade (about 90 percent) has the close mouth. AA dried fig is the most favorable type for export, due to being competitive in price. In addition, A type of dried fig with 14mm size has the most reasonable price and lowest quality in Iranian dried figs.

OPEN MOUTH

The most exclusive and expensive type among all Iranian dried figs is the big size of open mouth dried figs. AAA grade of open mouth fig is in white color or slightly yellow. This type is big in size and majority of figs of this type are the open mouth, which makes them easy to eat and chew. The size of each fig in this type should not be less than 22 mm and close mouth figs are not allowed to be more than 10%. AA is the medium size and A grade of dried fig is the smallest size with brownish color.
